Drivers of state fleet vehicles must have a valid driver’s license and be over 18 years of age. Student Organizations will not be permitted to use University vehicles unless a faculty, staff or student employee member is driving the vehicle. Vehicle keys are available at the Transportation Services office during normal business hours (8:00 am to 5:00 pm) Monday through Friday.
